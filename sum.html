Project Summary

Our visualizations help get insights to questions like:
What Campaign source works best?
From our visualizations we observed that google ads work best in terms of attracting visitors.
Services like Refinance are attracting more visitors as opposed to Business Loans because there are more google ads for Refinance compared to Business Loans.
Which Product Reporting is popular in each state?
From this visualization, it looks like Refinance is a popular service that users are visiting the website for.
This could be related to the previous visualization on csource. If you were to google the word “refinance”, you will see links that mention LendingTree. But if you google “Business Loans”, you will see that LendingTree is not mentioned in the first few links that the user sees.
